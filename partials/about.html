What is CoraOS.

CoraOS is an independent Fedora Linux-based distribution designed for users who want the raw speed and control of Linux with improved ease of use.

CoraOS comes in three editions: Default Edition for general use, Developer Edition packed with coding tools and productivity enhancements, and Gaming Edition optimized for gaming performance.

The core distribution is 100% free and open-source. We also offer a Premium Version with exclusive features for power users.

CoraOS is an independent project and is not affiliated with Red Hat, Inc., Fedora, Microsoft, Valve, or SteamOS.
