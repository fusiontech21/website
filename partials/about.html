What is CoraOS.

CoraOS is an independent Arch Linux-based distribution designed for users who want the raw speed and control of Linux — without the complexity. Built on one of the most powerful kernels available, every single component is tuned and tweaked to squeeze out maximum performance.

Unlike anything else out there, CoraOS is designed for everyone. You don't need to know what a terminal is. You don't need to read a wiki. Everything just works. Every app, every setting, every detail — crafted with love and obsessive attention to detail.

When something breaks, you shouldn't need to be an expert to fix it. CoraOS detects the issue automatically and presents you with a simple menu — fix it yourself, open a terminal, troubleshoot, or hit one button and your system goes back to exactly how it was. No terminal. No googling. No panic.

CoraOS comes in two editions: a Free Edition for everyone, and a Premium Edition for power users who want even more. A Server Edition is also in development, featuring an optimized LTS kernel, one-click security lockdown, intrusion detection, and a built-in dashboard.

Fast enough for power users. Safe enough for everyone else. This is Linux, done right.
