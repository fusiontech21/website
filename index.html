What is CoraOS?

CoraOS is an Arch Linux-based distribution designed for users who want the raw speed and control of Arch, paired with the ease of use of Ubuntu.

I created CoraOS to help users switching from Windows experience the power and performance of Arch with total ease.

The installer includes a dedicated Gaming Option that, when selected, automatically installs essential gaming packages and optimizes your system for peak performance right out of the box.

The core distribution is 100% free and open-source. However, we also offer a Premium Version with exclusive features and additional perks for power users.
